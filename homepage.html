Time Manage

Managing your time for you, so you don't have to

Ever felt like you have no free time in your life? Lots of Americans feel that way, but that's only because they haven't planned their day properly. That could include spending too much time on your phone to procrastinating certain things. This planner will help you solve these problems. Just enter your activies and how long they take, and we will create a personalized planner for you, allocating for free time that you would like. Or, you could enter your schedule manually and we will send you notifications to remind you what and when to do.
